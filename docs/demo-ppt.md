## 1. Title Slide
# EchoRank V2
### Listen to the Community Pulse: Emotion-Aware Decentralized Message Board

---

## 2. Pain Points: The "Oblivion Trap"
*   **Ephemeral**: Great discussions vanish once the event ends.
*   **Disorganized**: Traditional message boards lack structure and meaningful categorization.
*   **Filtering Burden**: Organizers struggle to sieve through noise for high-quality insights.
*   **Privacy Concerns**: Users fear sharing raw voice data on public clouds due to biometric risks.

---

## 3. The Solution: AI-Driven Interactive Loop
*   **Smart Filter**: Gemini-powered validation for "Local, Web3, and Co-creation" criteria.
*   **The Ear**: **Locally-running** SenseVoice AI for instant emotion and keyword detection.
*   **The Brain**: Smart aggregation of fragmented feedback into 100% anonymized consensus reports.
*   **Auditability**: SHA256-based voice fingerprinting for verifiable off-chain storage.

---

## 4. Demo Flow: Simple, Authentic, Deep
1.  **Fast Submit**: Paste a Luma link; AI performs instant background validation.
2.  **Natural Feedback**: No typing needed. Speak to the bot; it detects laughter and tone.
3.  **Auto-categorize**: Group messages by sentiment (Excitement, Touching, Wind-storm).
4.  **Consensus Report**: `/report` generates actionable insights from collective voices.

---

## 5. Technology Hedge: Local & Production Ready
*   **100% Privacy**: Raw audio is processed on the local nodeâ€”**Never leaves the device**.
*   **One-Click Deployment**: Docker-based setup; any Mac becomes a sovereign AI hub.
*   **Hybrid Efficiency**: Local lightweight AI (High-frequency audio) + Cloud Logic AI.
*   **Scalable Architecture**: Hub-and-Spoke model supporting multiple community bots.

---

## 6. Future Roadmap
*   **On-chain Identity**: Connect verifiable credentials to record "Emotional Contributions".
*   **Gasless NFTs**: Auto-mint commemorative NFTs for high-resonance feedback.
*   **Decentralized Verification**: Integrate multi-node consensus to ensure feedback credibility.
